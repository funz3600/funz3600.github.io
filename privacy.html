Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how YTCommunity500Bot ("the Bot," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information obtained from users ("you") when you interact with the Bot.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize the Bot via Google OAuth, we collect and store:

  • Your Telegram User ID
  • Your YouTube Channel ID and public channel name
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens (used to perform subscription actions on your behalf)

We do not collect your Google account password, email content, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To link your Telegram account with your YouTube channel.
  • To allow you to subscribe to other members' YouTube channels with a single click.
  • To allow other members to subscribe to your YouTube channel (only if you have opted in by connecting your account).

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in a secure SQLite database on Render's cloud infrastructure. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your YouTube channel name is displayed to other Bot users only when you have connected your account, to facilitate the subscription feature.

6. Your Rights & Revoking Access

You may revoke the Bot's access to your YouTube account at any time by visiting your Google Account Permissions page and removing the Bot. Upon revocation, your stored tokens will no longer function. You may also contact us to request deletion of your data from our database.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact
