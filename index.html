This is the official homepage for the Telegram bot @YTCommunity500Bot.

What does this bot do?

This bot allows members of the Fun size Telegram community to connect their YouTube channels and support each other with one-click subscriptions. When you authorize the bot, it securely stores your YouTube channel information and can subscribe you to other members' channels directly from Telegram.

How it works

  1. Join the Fun size Telegram group and start the bot with /start.
  2. Click "Connect YouTube" and sign in with your Google account.
  3. Grant permission to manage your YouTube subscriptions (this is the only permission requested).
  4. Use /channels to see other members' channels and subscribe with one click.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. The bot only requests access to manage your YouTube subscriptions. It does not access your Google password, emails, or other personal data. Your OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only for the subscription actions you initiate.
